About

UI/UX Designer / Design Team Lead / Web Designer

Hi! I’m Dako, 35 years old. When I was in university, I focused on art and graphic design. After I graduated from college, I started working as a web and media designer. I designed responsive web pages, improved on the UX/UI of eCommerce site. In recent years, I have mainly focused on mobile product design.

In my previous job, I worked in Paradise Soft company as a design manager, led a team of more than 12 UI/UX designers. I was mainly in charge of product design, cross-departmental communication and building the design department. I met a great partner here. This work experience increases my skills and knowledge. At the same time,I accumulated a lot of experience in design and management. With all the skills and experience, i believe that I can do the best for everything in my next job.
